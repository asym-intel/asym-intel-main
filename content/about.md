Asymmetric Intelligence is a free, public OSINT intelligence commons publishing weekly structured intelligence briefings across seven monitored domains: AI governance, democratic integrity, European strategic autonomy, global macro, FIMI & cognitive warfare, environmental risks & planetary boundaries, and strategic conflict & escalation.

Each domain is tracked by a dedicated monitor publishing one briefing per week on a fixed schedule. All output is open, sourced, and permanent.

Read the [Mission & Principles](/mission/) to understand what this platform does, what it will not do, and the analytical standards that govern every monitor.
