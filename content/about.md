Asymmetric Intelligence is a public intelligence commons publishing weekly executive briefings across seven monitored domains: AI governance, democratic integrity, European strategic autonomy, global macro, FIMI & cognitive warfare, environmental risks & planetary boundaries, and strategic conflict & escalation.

Each domain is tracked by a dedicated monitor publishing one briefing per week on a fixed day.
