*Ten analytical threads stand out from the week of 24 March 2026.*

## 1. V-Dem 2026 Quantifies the US Collapse

The [Democracy Report (published March 17)](https://v-dem.net) provides the most authoritative single data point of the week: the United States' Liberal Democracy Index fell from 0.75 to 0.57 in one year — a 24% decline, the largest annual drop in US history, dropping the world's most influential democracy from 20th to 51st in global rankings. Severity score: **5.5↑**. The report explicitly identifies six of the ten new autocratizing countries as being in Europe and North America. V-Dem's Lindberg: "several of these countries have the economic and political weight to reshape international organisations, norms, and trade, effectively reshaping the global order."
