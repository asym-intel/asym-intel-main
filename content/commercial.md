Asymmetric Intelligence is a free, public intelligence commons — and the engine that powers it is a full-stack intelligence operating system. The same pipeline architecture that runs seven open monitors also drives commercial intelligence products across regulated industries, geopolitical risk, and institutional analysis.

This page describes what is available commercially. The open platform remains free and editorially independent.

## The Engine

The asym engine transforms open-source information into structured, trust-labelled intelligence through a multi-stage pipeline: automated collection, AI-assisted analysis, human editorial oversight, and deterministic publication. Every stage produces versioned, machine-readable output with source attribution and confidence scoring.

<div style="margin-bottom:0.5rem"><a href="https://advennt.io" style="color:var(--color-primary,#01696f);font-size:0.9rem">advennt.io</a></div>

### Leverage Signal — Geopolitical Risk and Macro Intelligence
<div style="margin-bottom:0.5rem;color:var(--color-text-secondary,#7a7974);font-size:0.85rem">In development</div>

A weekly intelligence service adding structured risk factors, market context, and forward-looking implications to the geopolitical analysis produced by the open platform. Designed for investors, strategists, and institutional risk functions who need to understand what current developments may mean for portfolios and operations over a 6-24 month horizon.

### Financial Integrity Monitor
<div style="margin-bottom:0.5rem;color:var(--color-text-secondary,#7a7974);font-size:0.85rem">In development</div>

Weekly OSINT intelligence tracking illicit financial flows, sanctions-evasion architecture, beneficial-ownership opacity, and enabler-jurisdiction dynamics. Five analytical domains covering sanctions, corporate transparency, conflict finance, facilitator networks, and digital asset integrity. Natural audience: compliance teams, sanctions advisory firms, and investigative analysts.
