## CASCADE DELEGATION

This monitor's interpretation, composition, and review logic live in the dedicated
cascade artefacts: `interpreter-prompt.txt`, `composer-prompt.txt`, `reviewer-prompt.txt`.

- **Interpreter** (`interpreter-prompt.txt`): reasons over weekly-research documents;
  produces structured interpretation JSON (wdm-interpret-v1.0) with 16 thematic modules,
  structured_claims[] triple store, cycle_disposition, and gaps_register[].
- **Composer** (`composer-prompt.txt`): consumes interpret-latest.json; renders the
  800-1200 word weekly_brief_draft under hard provenance constraints; emits claim_trace[].
- **Reviewer** (`reviewer-prompt.txt`): gates the Interpreter output before Composer
  dispatch; produces advisory_notes[] for Composer awareness; verdict is deterministic
  (rule engine, not LLM).

The Synthesiser stage is the upstream-research anchor. It continues to receive and
structure the weekly research bundle as input. Downstream, the Interpreter consumes
the Synthesiser's output document set. The output contract (synthesiser-schema.json)
remains the authoritative schema for the Synthesiser's structured output.

## CONFIDENCE TIER STANDARD (cross-monitor canonical — v1.0, approved 16 Apr 2026)

Four tiers apply to all main signal confidence fields and key_judgments.
Use exactly these labels — no synonyms.

| Tier | Label | Corroboration standard |
|------|-------|----------------------|
| 1 | Confirmed | 2+ independent sources, at least one T1-T2. No active contradicting evidence. |
| 2 | High | Single strong T1-T3 source, or 2+ T3-T4 sources consistent. No direct contradiction. |
| 3 | Assessed | Credible T3-T4 evidence. Analytical judgment supported but not independently corroborated. Default. |
| 4 | Possible | Min 2 independent source categories. Pattern-consistent but evidence thin, indirect, or early. |

key_judgments use three tiers only: High | Assessed | Possible.
Auto-downgrade rule: if confidence == "Confirmed" but research_traceback lacks a T1-T2 source, downgrade to "High".
