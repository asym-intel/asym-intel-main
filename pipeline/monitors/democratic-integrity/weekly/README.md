# WDM Weekly Research Output

**Monitor:** World Democracy Monitor (democratic-integrity)
**Layer:** Weekly Deep Research (GitHub Actions)
**Model:** sonar-pro (live web search, deeper synthesis)
**Schedule:** Sunday 18:00 UTC (12 hours before WDM Analyst at Monday 06:00 UTC)

## Files

- `weekly-latest.json` — most recent weekly research run (overwritten weekly)
- `weekly-YYYY-MM-DD.json` — dated archive

## Purpose

Comprehensive weekly research sweep across V-Dem, CIVICUS, Freedom House,
OSCE ODIHR, Venice Commission, RSF, and investigative outlets covering
democratic backsliding, institutional erosion, electoral events, and
autocratic export patterns.

The WDM Analyst reads this at Step 0D each Monday as the primary
research base, supplemented by Step 0C (daily candidates).
