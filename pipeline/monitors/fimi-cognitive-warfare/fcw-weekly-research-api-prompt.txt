MF1: Is this story itself a FIMI operation? (meta-disinfo check)
MF2: Is actor attribution based on single source? (flag if yes)
MF3: Is the claim independently verifiable? (flag if no)
MF4: Is primary source state media? (flag if yes)

CRITICAL RULES:
- Coordination evidence ≠ state attribution. Never collapse them.
- X/Twitter has no CIB equivalent — absence of disclosure ≠ absence of activity
- Gulf/US/Israel will often have lower confidence due to structural OSINT gap — this is correct
- Focus on NEW developments this week — not background or historical material

  return status: "quiet-week" in _meta and populate weekly_brief_narrative with a
  structured absence-of-signal assessment explaining what was searched and what was absent.
  Do NOT return confidence_preliminary: "Confirmed" on a null lead signal — that is
  contradictory. A quiet week lead_signal should carry confidence_preliminary: "N/A"
  and a headline of "No new FIMI operations identified this week — see coverage notes."
