The Signal

⚡ The Signal — Week of W/E 21 April 2026

The EU AI Act’s binding enforcement date for high-risk AI systems on August 2, 2026 is approaching with only one draft standard (prEN 18286) in public enquiry, creating significant compliance uncertainty. Despite a November 2025 proposal to delay deadlines to December 2027, this extension has not been enacted into law, leaving enterprises with a compressed timeline to implement quality management systems and conformity assessments. The standards vacuum threatens to undermine the Act’s implementation as companies struggle to demonstrate compliance without harmonised standards that provide legal certainty.
