Commercial Services

Structured intelligence products, bespoke analysis, and the engine behind them — available for your organisation.

Asymmetric Intelligence is a free, public intelligence commons — and the engine that powers it is a full-stack intelligence operating system. The same pipeline architecture that runs seven open monitors also drives commercial intelligence products across regulated industries, geopolitical risk, and institutional analysis.

This page describes what is available commercially. The open platform remains free and editorially independent.

The Engine

The asym engine transforms open-source information into structured, trust-labelled intelligence through a multi-stage pipeline: automated collection, AI-assisted analysis, human editorial oversight, and deterministic publication. Every stage produces versioned, machine-readable output with source attribution and confidence scoring.

Domain-agnostic
The same pipeline structure works for geopolitical risk, regulatory monitoring, financial compliance, or any structured knowledge domain. Only the entity set and analytical prompts change.
Structured and machine-readable
All output is versioned JSON with trust labels, confidence levels, and source hierarchy. Ready for API consumption, institutional reporting, and AI discovery from day one.
Hybrid human-AI editorial
Two-tier trust model: pipeline output is published as "AI Unverified" and can be promoted to "Verified" after expert review. Published methodology builds the credibility that commands subscription prices.
Low operating cost
Near-zero infrastructure cost on free tiers. AI spend is in collection and analysis, not publication. A new intelligence vertical can be deployed in days, not months.
